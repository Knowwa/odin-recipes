Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

There's always time for Pizza!

A simple baked dish made of flattened bread dough with a savory mixture of tomatoes and cheese that is hard to master. If only it could be delivered on time!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Bread Flour 00
  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 1 Tbsp. of Active Dry Yeast
  • 1 Tbsp. of Honey
  • 1 can of San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Slices of Mozzarella Cheese
  • One package of Pepperoni
  • 2 Tbsp. of Olive Oil
  • 1.5 Tbsp. of Salt
  • A few Slices of Roman Tomatoes

Instructions for four 13 inch Pizzas

  1. Mix Flour, Water, Honey, Active Dry Yeast, Olive Oil, and Salt. Kneed until play-dough texture.
  2. Oil Bowl, place dough ball in, and place kitchen towel on top. Let it rise for 2 hours.
  3. After 2 hours seal and place in fridge to use the next day.
  4. When dough is ready separate into as many 140 gram dough balls you can get.
  5. At this point you should prepare your San marzano tomatoes by blending them into a sauce.
  6. Now you are ready to assemble! Flatten dough ball with hands or roller and bake at 500celsius until done.
